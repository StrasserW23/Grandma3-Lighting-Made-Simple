Huge Screen Real Estate: Massive multi-touch screens give you total control.

Work Without Boundaries

The grandMA3 breaks LAN limitations with powerful WAN capabilities. LAN vs. WAN: The high-performance, real-time DMX output (MA-Net3) still requires a dedicated 1 Gbit/s LAN, but the console's overall system management can leverage WAN.

LAN vs. WAN

LAN (Local Area Network) = fast, short-distance communication → like talking to someone in the same room

WAN (Wide Area Network) = long-distance communication → like talking to someone over the internet
